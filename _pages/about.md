I am now a second year Ph.D. student in [THUIR](http://www.thuir.cn/), [Department of Computer Science and Technology](http://www.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn) in Tsinghua University, Beijing, China. My supervisors are Prof. Shaoping Ma and Prof. [Yiqun Liu](http://www.thuir.cn/group/~YQLiu/). My major research interests lie in neural ranking.
